About

My name is Ray Winardi. I like:

  • programming
  • karaoke
  • sleeping during the day

I'm currently pursuing my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at McMaster University. I am well acquainted with Python, PHP and ELM. Over the summer of 2016, I was heavily involved in my school's Computer and Software Outreach program, teaching kids functional programming to make platformer games and fantastic presentations.

On the side, I'm a cast member of the McMaster Engineering Musical and fully intend on continuing that trend for the rest of my tenure at McMaster. (I also helped design the website!)

Projects

Here are some projects I have written in ELM. (Note: the platformers were written for ELM 0.16, before the removal of signals.) If you are interested in seeing the source code, they are all available in my public repositories on my Github Page.

Let's put aside all the complicated integrals and derivatives we've learnt to go back to a simpler time, when we only needed to know four basic operations. Try this little game which turns simple math into a puzzling platformer.

MATH PLATFORMER

Trivia games. Been there, done that. This game demands you answer a question and spell it out correctly by jumping from platform to platform with the corresponding characters. It's a lot like math platformer, but there is far more room for ingenuity.

WORD PLATFORMER

This was the result of a book presentation I had to do in my first-year going into University. I love reading about this topic, and consistently go back to these slides of mine to add new information or improve the animations. Take a look!

BIG DATA SLIDES
