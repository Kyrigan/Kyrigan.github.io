Introducing the MemeStick™

Flood any computer device on the planet with PNGs of internet humour.

Who are we?

We are Net Enthusiasts, a group of internet lovers who take delight in the 'dankness' of the world wide web, and want to spread our memes everywhere we can. Our ideology revolves around humour, hardwork and having a wonderful time brightening someone else's day.

So what is it?

On the surface, the MemeStick™ looks like an average flash drive, but it contains several powerful programs which can quickly overwhelm a storage drive with gigabytes upon terabytes of memes.

The MemeStick™ has different settings, depending on how much you wish to troll your target. You can either have it replace all their images with your top quality internet humour, or have the stick completely uninstall the target device's operating system, only to replace it with our custom-made MemeOS™ built off the Unix platform.
